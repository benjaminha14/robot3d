Team 254

Mission

To enhance the awareness of STEM education and FIRST robotics, Team 254 provides demonstrations of our robots to the community at large. We expose the public to the wonders of STEM at Bellarmine College Preparatory, local colleges including Stanford and Santa Clara University, and many other events.
